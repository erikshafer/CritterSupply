# CritterSupply — Session Guide

Welcome! You've been given access to **CritterSupply**, a pet supply online store. CritterSupply is where pet owners come to buy food, toys, accessories, and supplies for their animals.

**Your role today:** You are *Alice*, a returning customer who has an existing account with CritterSupply. You have a dog and a cat at home and you're doing a bit of online shopping.

Please **think out loud** as you go. Say what you're looking for, what you expect to happen, and what confuses you. There are no wrong answers — your honest reactions are the whole point.

## Your Tasks

Work through these tasks in order. Take your time. If something doesn't work the way you expect, note it — and move on to the next task.

---

### Task 1 — Sign In

Open the CritterSupply website and sign in using the email and password above.

> **You're done when:** You can see the main page and it feels like you're logged in.

---

### Task 2 — Get Your Bearings

You've just logged in. Look around the site.

- What does the homepage tell you about CritterSupply?
- Figure out what you can do from here.
- Find the main navigation — where can you go?

> **You're done when:** You feel oriented and know where to start shopping.

---

### Task 3 — Browse for a Pet Product

You're looking for something for your dog. Browse the product catalog.

- Find the section or filter for dog products.
- Look at what's available. Do the product listings give you enough information to decide?

> **You're done when:** You've browsed at least a few products and could identify something to buy.

---

### Task 4 — Add Items to Your Cart

You've decided you want:
- A **dog bowl** (or whatever dog product looks good to you)
- One other item of your choice

Add both to your cart.

> **You're done when:** You've added two items and feel like they're in your cart.

---

### Task 5 — Review Your Cart

Go to your shopping cart and review what's in it.

- Are the items correct?
- Does the total look right?
- Try adjusting the quantity of one item.

> **You're done when:** Your cart looks right and you're ready to buy.

---

### Task 6 — Start Checkout

You're ready to buy. Begin the checkout process from your cart.

Work through each step as it comes — the site will guide you. Use whatever information the site asks for.

> **Note:** For the payment step, use this as your payment information: `tok_visa_test_12345`

> **You're done when:** You've completed all the checkout steps and submitted your order.

---

### Task 7 — Confirm Your Order

You should now see an order confirmation.

- What information do you see?
- Does the confirmation give you enough confidence that your order went through?
- Is there anything you'd want to know that isn't shown?

> **You're done when:** You've read the confirmation page and feel like your purchase is complete.

---

### Task 8 — Find Your Past Orders

You'd like to review your order history — the orders you've placed with CritterSupply.

Find that section and see what's there.

> **You're done when:** You've found the order history and looked at what's available.

---

### Task 9 — Check Your Account Details

Find your account information — your name, email, and any account details.

> **You're done when:** You've located your account information.

---

### Task 10 — Sign Out

You're done shopping for the day. Sign out of your account.

> **You're done when:** You are no longer logged in.
