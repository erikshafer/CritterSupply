# Vendor Portal — Session Guide

Welcome! You've been given access to CritterSupply's **Vendor Portal** — the platform that CritterSupply uses to work directly with its product suppliers.

**Your role today:** You are a representative from *Acme Pet Supplies*, a pet product supplier that has recently started selling through CritterSupply. You have full access to manage your company's presence on the platform.

Please **think out loud** as you go. Say what you're looking for, what you expect to happen, and what confuses you. There are no wrong answers — your honest reactions are the whole point.

## Your Tasks

Work through these tasks in order. Take your time. If something doesn't work the way you expect, note it — and move on to the next task.

---

### Task 1 — Sign In

Open the Vendor Portal website and sign in using the email and password above.

> **You're done when:** You can see the main dashboard with your company name visible.

---

### Task 2 — Get Your Bearings

You've just logged in for the first time. Look around the dashboard.

- What does the dashboard tell you about your business on CritterSupply?
- Find out what role your account has.
- Locate the section that would let you navigate to other parts of the portal.

> **You're done when:** You feel oriented and ready to take action.

---

### Task 3 — Submit a Product Change Request

Your team has updated the description for one of your products and you need to get it updated on CritterSupply's side. The product SKU is **ACME-DOG-001**.

Submit a change request to update the product description. Use this as the new description:

> *Premium all-natural dog chew made from sustainably sourced beef hide. No artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. Available in three sizes: small, medium, and large. Suitable for all breeds over 6 months.*

---

### Task 4 — Save It as a Draft First

Before submitting, think about whether you want to review it one more time. Try saving the request as a draft instead of submitting it immediately.

> **You're done when:** The draft appears somewhere in the portal.

---

### Task 5 — Find Your Saved Draft and Submit It

Locate the draft you just saved and submit it for review.

> **You're done when:** The status of the request shows that it's been submitted for review.

---

### Task 6 — Check the Status of Your Request

Your request has been submitted. Where would you go to check what happened to it?

Navigate to that location and see what information is available.

---

### Task 7 — Adjust Your Notification Preferences

You'd like to receive notifications when your change requests are approved or rejected, but you don't want to receive low stock alerts right now.

Find the notification settings and make that adjustment.

---

### Task 8 — Sign Out

Your session is done for the day. Sign out of the portal.

> **You're done when:** You are back at the login page.
